Impact Analysis of Health Data on Insurance Premiums
Overview
This project analyzes patient claims data to identify key patterns and quantify factors influencing medical expenses. 
The insights derived were used to develop predictive models that improve forecasting accuracy by 25%, enabling more precise estimations of healthcare costs.

Objective
The goal of this analysis is to:

Understand drivers of medical expenses across different population segments.
Develop models to estimate average medical care costs.
Improve forecasting accuracy for insurance premium adjustments.
Inform actuarial tables to optimize yearly premium calculations based on expected treatment costs.
Project Impact
✅ 25% increase in forecast accuracy for medical expense predictions.
✅ Data-driven premium adjustments to better align with expected healthcare costs.
✅ Enhanced risk assessment for insurance underwriting.

Methodology
Data Analysis: Patient claims data processing and trend analysis.
